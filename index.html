About us

Welcome to Kabir Khaja Ghar —
a little piece of Nepal in Madhyapur Thimi!

We bring you authentic Newari cuisine made with love and traditional recipes. Our menu features aromatic curries, crispy samosas, flavorful tandoori dishes, and delicate desserts, all prepared with the finest spices and fresh ingredients.

Every dish is a journey through rich flavors and aromas, blending freshness, spice, and deep-rooted traditions. We use only natural ingredients and authentic Nepali spices to ensure a truly genuine taste experience.

Come and enjoy a warm atmosphere, great hospitality, and an unforgettable culinary adventure. We look forward to welcoming you and sharing the taste of Newar with you!
